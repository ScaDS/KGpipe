The Hobbit is a fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien, first published in 1937.
It follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, a reluctant hobbit who is swept into an adventure with a group of dwarves and the wizard Gandalf to reclaim treasure guarded by the dragon Smaug. Along the way, Bilbo faces trolls, goblins, elves, giant spiders, and discovers a mysterious ring that will shape the fate of Middle-earth.
